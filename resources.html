Packing

  • Books

  • Back, Joe. Horses, Hitches & Rocky Trails; the Packers Bible. A complete, often humorous presentation of packing horses into the wilderness, amplified by beautiful, often witty, drawings, and step-by-step instructions. ©1959, revised 2018.
  • Davis, Francis W. Horse Packing in Pictures. Everything to know about packing a horse with outstanding illustrations. © 1975.
  • Elser, Smoke and Bill Brown. Packin’ in on Mules and Horses. Professional outfitter’s system for safe, efficient backcountry packing. © 1980.
  • Harmon, Will. Leave No Trace, Minimum Impact Outdoor Recreation. The official manual of the American Hiking Society. The ethics and commonsense practices of backcountry human impact. Falcon Guide. ©1997.
  • Hill, Oliver C. Packing and Outfitting Field Manual. Basic information to plan and take pack trips with excellent knot and hitch tying diagrams, useful menus and equipment lists. 5 th edition, 2005.
  • Post, Charles Johnson. Horse packing: a manual of pack transportation. A practical guide with illustrations and written instructions on hitches, cordage, ropes, splices, knots and more for safe and efficient packing. © 2007.
  • Eversole, Robert. Horse and Mule Adventures with TrailMeister. Eversole helps horse and mule riders have a great time on the trail and in camp through videos, a book, articles, tips, a U.S. trail directory and in-person clinics.

Pack Stations (in alphabetical order)

BCHC provides this information as a resource and does not recommend or promote any pack station.

  • Central Coast Mountains

  • Johnston Horse Camp: Located in New Cuyama, CA, offers packing lessons, horse camping and mountain pack trips.

    Website: johnstonhorsecamp.com

  • Los Padres Outfitters: Located in Carpenteria, offers pack trips in the Santa Barbara Mountains.

    Website: lospadresoutfitters.com

  • Southern Sierra Nevada

  • Balch Park Pack Station: Located in Springville, CA, offers custom tailored horseback trips through remote High Sierra Wilderness.

    Website: balchpark.com
    Facebook: Balch Park Pack Station

  • Golden Trout Pack Station: Located in Springville, CA, provides pack trips in the Kern River Canyon, far into the Golden Trout Wilderness.

    Website: goldentroutpacktrains.com

  • Eastern Sierra Nevada

  • Bishop Pack Outfitters: Located in North Lake, CA, provides backcountry trips to Sabrina Basin, Horton Basin, Lamarck Lakes and Humphries Basin.

    Website: bishoppackoutfitters.net
    Facebook: Bishop Pack Outfitters

  • Cottonwood Pack Station: Located in Lone Pine, CA, offers supported hiking trips and will transport your gear to/from a point you specify or transport you AND your gear by horse/mule to/from a campsite).

    Website: cottonwoodpackstation.com

  • Frontier Pack Station: Located in June Lake, CA, takes people on trail rides and pack trips to lakes over 9,000 feet in elevation.

    Website: frontierpacktrain.com

  • Glacier Pack Train: Located in Big Pine, CA, provides day rides and longer stays at

    Website: glacierpacktrain.com

  • Leavitt Meadow Pack Station: Located on the Eastern base of Sonora Pass off 108, guided trips into the Hoover wilderness and beautiful destinations up to the Northern Borders of Yosemite.

    Website: leavittmeadows.com
    Facebook: Leavitt Meadows

  • Mammoth Lakes Pack Outfit: Located in Mammoth Lakes, CA, offers custom pack trips and a myriad of services with options ranging from all-inclusive trips.

    Website: mammothpack.wixsite.com
    Facebook: Mammoth Lakes Pack Outfit

  • McGee Creek Pack Station: Located near Mammoth Lakes, CA, offers guided trips into the John Muir Wilderness and other scenic areas of the Eastern Sierra.

    Website: mcgeecreekpackstation.com
    Facebook: McGee Creek Pack Station

  • Red’s Meadow Pack Station: Operating out of Reds Meadow Valley near Mammoth Lakes, offers guided trips into the Ansel Adams Wilderness and other areas in the vicinity.

    Website: redsmeadow.com
    Facebook: Red's Meadow Pack Station

  • Rainbow Pack Outfitters: Located west of Bishop behind Parchers Resort. A working family business operated since 1924, provides excellent service into the Eastern Sierra backcountry.

    Website: rainbowpackoutfitters.com

  • Rock Creek Pack Station: Situated in the eastern Sierra near Bishop, organizes pack trips into the beautiful wilderness areas surrounding Rock Creek and the John Muir Wilderness.

    Website: rockcreekpackstation.com
    Facebook: Rock Creek Pack Station

  • Virginia Lakes Pack Outfit: Based near Bridgeport, provides guided trips into the Hoover Wilderness, Emigrant Wilderness, and other scenic locations.

    Website: virginialakes.com
    Facebook: Virginia Lakes Pack Outfit

  • West Central Sierra & Yosemite

  • Aspen Meadow Pack Station, Inc.: Located near Dodge Ridge Ski Area and Pinecrest resort has been guiding riders, hunters and anglers to the Emigrant Wilderness area for more than 80 years.

    Website: aspenmeadowpackstation.com
    Facebook: Aspen Meadow Pack Station

  • Clyde Pack Outfitters: Outfitter and guide service with pack stations out of Dinkey Creek, Courtright and Wishon lakes, serving the John Muir Wilderness.

    Website: clydepackoutfit.com
    Facebook: Clyde Pack Outfitters

  • D & F Pack Station: Located in Lakeshore, CA, offers backcountry overnight pack trips in the Shaver Basin, halfway between Yosemite and Kings Canyon National Parks.

    Website: dfpackstation.co
    Facebook: D&F Pack Station

  • Horse Corral Pack Station: Located between Sequoia and Kings Canyon National Parks, offers day rides, overnight pack trips, and resupplies (we haul freight on four legs).

    Facebook: Horse Corral Pack Station

  • High Sierra Pack Station: Located in Lakeshore, CA, offers pack trips for all outdoor lovers: hikers, fishermen, and sightseers.

    Website: highsierrapackstations.com
    Facebook: High Sierra Pack Station

  • Kennedy Meadows Pack Station: Situated near Sonora Pass in the central Sierra, provides guided trips into the Emigrant Wilderness, Carson-Iceberg Wilderness, and other scenic areas.

    Website: kennedymeadows.com
    Facebook: Kennedy Meadows Resort

  • Minarets Pack Station: Located on the southern border of Yosemite National Park provides pack service into the Ansel Adam's Wilderness and Southern Yosemite National Park.

    Website: minaretspack.com
    Facebook: Minarets Pack Station

  • Muir Trail Ranch: Lakeshore, CA, offers horse-assisted trips into the Sierra National Forest and Kings Canyon National Park.

    Website: muirtrailranch.com
    Facebook: Muir Trail Ranch

  • Sequoia Kings Pack Trains, DBA Pine Creek Pack Station and Berners' Pack Outfits

    Website: sequoiakingspacktrains.weebly.com
    Facebook: Sequoia Kings Pack Trains

  • Lake Tahoe & Nothern Sierra

  • Reid Horse & Cattle Co.:With operations based in Plumas County, CA, offers pack trips from their Gold Lake Pack Station.

    Website: reidhorse.com
