To read the Back Country Horsemen of America newsletter online, go to: https://www.bcha.org/blog/newsletter/

Backcountry News is the quarterly publication of Backcountry Horsemen of California. Annual subscription is free for BCHC members. Annual digital and print subscriptions are $15 per year for non-members. One free digital issue may be provided upon request.

Advertisers: Rates are reasonable and target a concentrated equestrian group. All ads must be paid in advance. Contact the newsletter editor for more information.

In our best efforts to go green and Leave No Trace, past editions are posted on this website.

If you would like to help BCHC save postage and printing costs by receiving a digitals subscription, please send your email address to BCHC VP of Membership. He will email a link to the electronic newsletter when it becomes available.
